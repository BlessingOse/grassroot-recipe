Yam Porridge Recipe

Description:

This Nigerian dish which is popularly known by the Yorubas as (ASARO) is surely one of the easiest dish anyone can make.

It tastes as beautiful as it looks if properly prepared with the right ingredients. Check below for the ingredients required to make a delicious yam porridge.

Ingredients:

  • Yam (Main)
  • Vegitable
  • Crayfish
  • Dry fish of fresh fish (Optional)
  • Palm Oil
  • Onion and Pepper
  • Ginger and Garlic (Optional)
  • Salt and seasoning to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Peel and cut the yam into preferred chunck size.
  3. Wash the yam and add into boiling water.
  4. Add salt to taste and let it cook for about 7mins.
  5. Add the blended tomatoes, onions, pepper, crayfish, ginger and garlic.
  6. Add any seasoning of your choice to taste.
  7. Let it cook for about 3mins.
  8. Add palm oil as you like it but be careful not to flood it with palm oil.
  9. Properly stir together.
  10. Add the already washed and sliced vegitable. You can put the cooker off imediately as the heat will be enough to cook the vegitable.

There you have it, your properly cooked and delicious yam porridge. Enjoy!
