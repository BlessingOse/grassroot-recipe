Black Soup Recipe

Description:

If you are looking for one soup that can as well pass for medicine, then this is it. "Black soup" as it it widely called by the Edo people of Nigeria is one of the healthiest soups that there is.

This soup is made with different natural plants/leaves that were graciously given to us by God, hence the so many health benefits.

Please note that in making this particular soup, there is no known fixed number or types of plants/leaves that must be used.

Ingredients:

  • Any type and number of leaves of your choice.
  • Banga sauce
  • Meat (any of your choice)
  • Salt and seasoning cubes to taste
  • Fish (dry or fresh fish)
  • Stock fish
  • Crayfish
  • Aligator pepper (Asi oto) Optional
  • Pepper and Onion
  • Locust beans (Iru)

Steps:

  1. If you are getting the banga juice from the fruits, then boil for about 30mins and pound to extract the juice. Extract the juice in a pot and put on the cooker. Here is a detailed video on how to extract the juice. How to make palm fruit extract.
  2. Or you can just get the already extracted juice at any supermarket close to you. Pour it into a pot and add water and place on the cooker.
  3. Blend your leaves and pour into your pot.
  4. Add your blended pepper, onions, crayfish, and aligator pepper.
  5. Go in with your boiled meats, dry fish and stock fish.
  6. Add you locust beans
  7. Let it cook for about 10mins.
  8. Add your salt and seasoning cubes to taste.
  9. If you are using any fresh fish, add them just few minutes before putting off your cooker so it does not get over cooked.

You can serve with any swallow of your choice. Enjoy!
